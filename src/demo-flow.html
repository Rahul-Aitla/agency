⚙️ How It Works

Walk through what happens when a client sends an email. ← Back to Dashboard

1

📨 Email arrives

Google Apps Script detects the new email and forwards it to your server

From Gmail Inbox
From: client@coffeebrand.com  |  Subject: New logo design
"Hey, we need a logo for our new coffee brand. Something minimal and modern. We're thinking earthy tones. Let us know what you need from us!"
$ [Gmail] New email from client@coffeebrand.com
$ [Webhook] POST /webhook/email → 200 OK
2

🧠 AI parses the request

Groq (LLaMA) extracts the 5 fields from the email — some are present, some are missing

Extracted Fields
What
Logo
Purpose
New coffee brand
Deadline
— Not provided
Brand References
Minimal and modern, earthy tones
Budget Range
— Not provided
$ [Groq] Parsed email via llama-3.3-70b-versatile
$ [Parse] Missing fields: deadline, budgetRange
3

📧 Auto-reply for missing info

The server sends a reply email asking only for what's missing

Outgoing Reply
To: client@coffeebrand.com  |  Subject: A few more details needed
Thanks! To get started, we need:
• Your deadline
• Your budget range

Reply to this email with the missing info and we'll take it from there!
$ [Email] Sent reply → client@coffeebrand.com
$ [Storage] Request saved (status: pending_info)
4

🔄 Client replies — fields are filled

The client responds, Groq re-parses, and now all 5 fields are present

Client Reply → Re-parsed
What
Logo
Purpose
New coffee brand
Deadline
End of next month
Brand References
Minimal and modern, earthy tones
Budget Range
$1k–$2k
$ [Webhook] Client reply received
$ [Groq] Re-parsed reply — all fields present
$ ✅ Request complete! Routing now...
5

🎯 Routed to the right person

The system matches the request type to the correct team member

✅
From email to assignment — fully automated. No human needed.
